Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to create a website?
A standard website can typically be launched within 4 to 6 weeks. The completion time can vary from a few weeks to several months, depending on the complexity of the project. We will work closely with you to establish a realistic timeline that meets your expectations.
Do you offer SEO services to improve site visibility?
Yes, we incorporate best SEO practices from the beginning of the design process to help improve your site's visibility on search engines. We also offer advanced SEO services for more comprehensive search engine optimization strategies.
Can we update the website ourselves once it's live?
We use user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update your site's content without requiring technical skills. We will also provide training on how to manage your site after its launch.
Do you offer web hosting and email addresses?
Yes, we offer secure and reliable web hosting solutions, as well as the setup of professional email addresses. These services can be included in our packages or offered separately according to your needs.
How do you secure websites against hacking?
Security is a priority for us. We implement robust security measures, including SSL certificates, malware protection, and regular backups to protect your site against online threats.
Will there be a maintenance contract? What services are included?
We offer maintenance contracts to ensure your site operates smoothly after its launch. This includes regular updates, security backups, and technical support to answer any questions you may have.
Can you integrate specific features, such as e-commerce, a blog, or forms?
We have experience integrating various features, such as online stores, blogs, and contact forms. Let's discuss your specific needs to create a customized solution that meets your objectives.
How does the design process work? Will we have a say in the design?
Our process begins by understanding your goals and preferences. We will provide initial mockups, and you will have the opportunity to provide feedback at each stage. Your participation is crucial to ensure the final design perfectly reflects your vision.
